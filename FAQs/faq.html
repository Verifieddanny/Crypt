Why did we form POLGA?

Many things could be improved about the cryptocurrency and blockchain ecosystem; ignorance and lack of proper education.
However, we understood the need for a reliable educational structure to dispense quality blockchain education; hence Play2Learn,
Crypto pidgin & Oluko Kiripto.

How is POLGA onboarding people into web3?

We onboard people into the web3 ecosystem through our hybrid games (Play2Learn) and web3 education in native languages
(Pidgin, Yoruba & Hausa).

Is POLGA a web3 company?

Polga is a web2.5 company serving as the bridge connecting both web2 and web3 industries.

Are your products only for newbies?

No, our products have no discrimination. It helps newbies familiarize themselves with the web3 ecosystem while allowing existing
web3 participants to have fun learning new things.
